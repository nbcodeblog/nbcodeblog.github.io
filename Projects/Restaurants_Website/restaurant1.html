Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk Solids
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom Pods
  • Rose Water
  • Oil or Ghee
  • Chopped Nuts
  • Chopped Raisins

Recipe

  1. Milk Solids: Khoya (evaporated milk) or milk powder is the main ingredient, providing the base for the jamun dough.
  2. All-Purpose Flour: Used to bind the milk solids and create a smooth, non-sticky dough.
  3. Baking Powder: Acts as a leavening agent, making the jamuns light and fluffy.
  4. Ghee (Clarified Butter): Added to the dough for flavor and to help the jamuns maintain their shape during frying.
  5. Sugar: Provides the sweet taste and helps create the syrup that the jamuns are soaked in.
  6. Water: Helps dissolve the sugar and create the syrup.
  7. Cardamom Pods: Add a fragrant aroma and flavor to the syrup.
  8. Rose Water: Adds a delicate floral fragrance and flavor to the syrup.
  9. Oil or Ghee: Used to deep fry the jamun balls until they turn golden brown.
  10. Chopped Nuts: Pistachios, almonds, or walnuts can be added for extra flavor and texture.
  11. Chopped Raisins: Can be added to enhance the flavor and texture of the jamuns.
